About Muzaffarpur

Muzaffarpur is located in the state of Bihar in India. It is famous for its agriculture, especially Litchi production.

The city lies on the bank of Burhi Gandak River.

Famous Tourist Places

  1. Ram Chandra Shahi Museum
  2. Baba Garib Nath Temple
  3. Jubba Sahni Park
  4. Khudiram Bose Memorial

Famous Food

  • Shahi Litchi
  • Litti Chokha
  • Thekua
  • Samosa
